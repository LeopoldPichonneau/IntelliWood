## Overview

The ambient sensing subsystem continuously monitors environmental conditions in the wood pellet storage room. Since pellets are flammable and sensitive to humidity, this subsystem plays a critical role for both storage quality and safety.

## Why monitor humidity?

High humidity degrades wood pellets and reduces their heating performance. By continuously monitoring temperature and relative humidity, IntelliWood allows intervention before pellets are damaged.

## Fire alert response

When an anomaly is detected (smoke, flame, or high CO2), the system immediately triggers push notifications to the companion mobile app.

The smoke and flame sensors work as a pair to minimize false positives:
- The MQ2 sensor detects combustible gases and smoke
- The infrared sensor confirms the presence of a flame
